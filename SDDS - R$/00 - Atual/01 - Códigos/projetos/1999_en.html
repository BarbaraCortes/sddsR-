SAE AeroDesign Brazil
 
 
AERObus

The AERObus was the first EESC-USP airplane to participate on a aerodesign competition. Its structure was made almost entirely out of wood, with the fuselage reinforced with a thin fiberglass shell and with a ribbed wing. Right on the beginning, the team won the first competition and was entitled to participate on the international competition the following year.
